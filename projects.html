WATCH FILM

'Oakman Pizza' is a horror comedy short film written and directed by Alex Chang and casted by McKenna Crenshaw that tells the story of a pizza delivery man who unwillingly stumbles into a cult meeting. The cast and crew of this film, shot in San Marcos California, is comprised of talented students attending Mission Hills High School.
